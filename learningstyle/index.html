Learning Style
By Tripp Lyons




What is a learning style?
A learning style is a way that one learns best.
4 major learning styles are:

Visual
Auditory
Read-Write
Kinesthetic

What is my most preffered learning style?
I consider myself a Read-Write learner.
Some ways that I learn best are:

Typing
Reading
Writing
Taking Notes
Coding
(I normally can't do this one, except for this assignment)

What is my least preffered learning style?
My least preferred learning style is Kinesthetic.
Some things that I can do to adjust to be Kinesthetic are:

Exercising
Meditating By Focusing On My Body
Doing Sports


Summary
All of us learn differently, but most of us fall into four different categories of learning styles:

Visual
Auditory
Read-Write
Kinesthetic

I work best as a Read-Write learner. For me this means that I learn best by:

Typing
Reading
Writing
Taking Notes
Coding
(Which I normally can't do except for this assignment)

I don't work as well as a Kinesthetic learner.
I can improve by:

Exercising
Meditating By Focusing On My Body
Doing Sports


Learning is important, and I hope to get better at it. I can do this by knowing my strengths and weaknesses. I hope to heighten my abilities in not one, but all learning styles.
